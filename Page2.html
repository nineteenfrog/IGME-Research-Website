Marketing In Videogames


eSports

What are eSports?
eSports stands for "electronic sports" or in other words, competitive video games. Video games can sometimes develop a competitive scene and then big eSports events are organised to support the scene. These events generate a lot of money and they bring in teams that compete to the international level for prize pools that sometimes get to the millions

Game companies invest in these competitions by providing proze pools for the people who compete and by hosting streams which also attract thousands of viewers. These also promote a game. People involved in a competitive game tend to be more involved in the game and also tend to promote it more. That is why game companies will always support a competitive scene for a game.
